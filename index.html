About

I am a PhD student at the Distributed Research on Emerging Applications and Machines Lab(DREAM Lab), Department of Computational and Data Sciences(CDS) at IISc. Broadly, my research focuses on scalable analytics for distributed edge computing. Previously, I worked on developing Graphics Device Drivers as part of Intel's Visual Technologies Team. I have an MTech in Embedded Systems from IIITB and a BTech in ECE from MSRIT. I'm passionate about everything in the hardware software intersection--OS, compilers, computer architecture, virtualization.
