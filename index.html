About

I am a systems researcher and PhD Candidate at the DREAM Lab, Department of Computational and Data Sciences, IISc. I work on the systems aspects of Edge AI, particularly on power and performance optimization, modeling and scheduling of DNN workloads on edge devices. My research has resulted in several poster, workshop, conference and journal publications at top international and national venues such as SIGMETRICS, FGCS, EuroSys, IPDPS, CCGRID and HiPC. Prior to my PhD, I worked on developing Graphics Device Drivers as part of Intel's Visual Technologies Team. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Communication from M S Ramaiah Institute of Technology and a Master's Degree in Electronic Systems Design from IIIT Bangalore, both with gold medals. I have received several awards, including the MLCommons' ML and Systems Rising Star Award, Prime Minister's Research Fellowship, Intel's Department Recognition Award, and travel grants from Microsoft Research, ACM-W, IEEE TCPP, USENIX, SIGMETRICS, EuroSys and CODS-COMAD.
